3 Vision Modes

Night Vision

Cats need 1/6th the light we do. See in the dark with 6x enhanced brightness and desaturated color.

UV Spectrum

Cats see ultraviolet light invisible to humans. Discover glowing patterns, fluorescent fabrics, and shimmering particles.

Predator Mode

Everything not moving gets grayed out. Targets pop with high contrast, magnification, and motion trails.

AI-Powered Features

Prey Detection

Body Heat Signatures

Full skeleton tracking with heat aura showing how cats sense warmth from living bodies.

Hand Tracking

Detects wiggling fingers as irresistible prey targets. Cats can't resist moving hands!

Depth Perception

AI depth estimation simulates a cat's superior 3D spatial awareness.

Enhanced Cat Senses

Dichromatic Color

Cats have no red cones. Reds become muted grayish-green while blues and greens are preserved.

2x Temporal Processing

Cats process motion twice as fast. Humans appear to move in slow motion with trailing afterimages.

"The Stare"

Reveals what cats see on blank walls: UV residue, micro-movements, particle drift, thermal prints.

Split-Screen Compare

Drag a divider to compare human vision alongside cat vision. Same scene, different realities.
